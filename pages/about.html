About Bustl.Cutz

Bustl Cutz app aims to allow everybody to use & operate their Apple shortcuts - the most powerful, modern framework for faster workflows & automations - without having to open the Shortcuts app.

  

Shortcuts, although a powerful tool even for those that don’t code, still requires some understanding of coding or the workflow of coding & can seem complex to the foreign eye.  We have received many feedback from our consumers that the interface of Apple’s Shortcuts app is complex enough to deter user to explore its features & functions.  

We want all iPhone users - regardless of their technical level or knowledge of coding, programming, or app development - to have a simple point of access, to their favorite shortcuts or our curated list of shortcuts, & benefit from all the amazing features the Shortcuts app can bring to our productivity.

Bustl Cutz actions include:

  • • Add a shortcut to the Bustl Cutz app
  • • Delete a shortcut from the Bustl Cutz app
  • • Trigger a shortcut from the Bustl Cutz app

Features in development:

  • — Download a shortcut from our curated Shortcuts library
  • — Reorder your shortcut menu
  • — Responsive design with number of columns dependent on screen size & number of shortcuts
